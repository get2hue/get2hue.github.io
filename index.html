Our Love Story

How We Met

I was supposed to meet up with another colleague so we all head to the restaurant for dinner to celebrate a friend’s birthday. After staying up to finish my assignment the night before I was completely exhausted by the time it came to getting ready. Once I was done I called my friends to ask where to meet up but no one answered. I had just given up, taken my heels off and was about to head home when the birthday girl called back.I weighed my options and thought, either go hang out with friends or have a lonely date on a Friday night with my computer.

First Date

Since it was during the Christmas holidays, Ugo went to see his family outside London so we were mostly calling and skyping throughout the holiday. Just 2 weeks after we met, he finally asked me out. We went out for a movie and spent the rest of the evening chatting. We went to our separate homes and ended up straight on Skype. That was the night he officially asked me to be his girlfriend. Although I liked him a lot I didn’t say yes straight away. C’mon I needed to make him sweat it out a little LOL. After a few more dates I finally agreed to be his girlfriend.

Engagement

2 years later we went for a friend’s wedding. All throughout that day he wasn’t himself. I asked many times if he was ok but he kept saying he was. I thought to give him space and when he was ready he will tell me what was bugging him. We went to the mall that day but I can’t remember what for. Out of the blue he said he wanted to go home to ease himself. Just like you are thinking I found it a bit strange seeing there were many restrooms around the mall. I mentioned it to him but he gave me a flimsy excuse.
